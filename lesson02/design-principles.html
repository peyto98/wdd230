Peyton White

Visual Hierarchy

ESPN

This website shows visual hierarchy as your eyes immediately go to the biggest news story. Then all around the big news story are different places you can go to find what you want.

White Space

Google

This website shows white space as there is lots of white space around the page. This is so you will focus on the search bar.

Rule of Thirds

Amazon

This website shows the rule of thirds with the top of the page having text, the middle of the page has a rectangle box that rotates through pictures, then it has boxes of different items.
